## The brief

Flour & Co. needed a site that felt as warm as their shop.
They wanted customers to easily find opening hours, browse the weekly menu, and contact them for custom orders.

## What I built

- Full-screen hero with an editorial warm colour palette
- Weekly specials section the client updates themselves via Markdown
- Simple contact form that emails directly to the owner
- Mobile-first layout for people checking on their phones nearby

## The outcome

Enquiries via the site increased in the first month.
The client updates their own specials every week with no help from me.
